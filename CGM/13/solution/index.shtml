The DCT converts one 8 x 8 block into frequency coefficients. The input samples are centered around zero before projection onto the cosine basis.

Quantization

Quantization divides every frequency coefficient by a table entry and rounds the result. This creates many zeros in high frequencies, which the provided run-length and Huffman encoder can store compactly.
