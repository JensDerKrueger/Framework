Shadow Matrices and Render Pass

The light uses its own projection and view matrices. The product with the clip-to-texture transform maps positions into shadow texture coordinates. Since the G-buffer stores positions in camera view space, the deferred shader receives viewToShadowMatrix.

Deferred Shadow Lookup

The deferred shader transforms the visible point into shadow-map coordinates and evaluates visibility with a 3 x 3 percentage-closer filter. The nine samples soften the shadow edge. Because this work happens in the full-screen deferred pass, it is paid once per visible pixel rather than during every geometry shader invocation.
