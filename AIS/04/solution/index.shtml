Tangent-Space Data

Normal mapping needs a tangent-space basis in addition to positions and normals. The vertex shader transforms normals, tangents, and binormals into view space and passes them to the fragment shader together with the texture coordinates.

Textured Ground Plane

The ground plane shader samples the diffuse and specular textures for kd and ks. The normal map is remapped from [0, 1] to [-1, 1], transformed by the TBN matrix, and used in the Phong lighting calculation.

Teapot Normal Mapping

The teapot uses the same tangent-space normal reconstruction, but keeps a fixed blue diffuse material color. Only the normal map changes the local lighting direction, which makes the logo appear engraved without changing the actual mesh geometry.

Rendering and Geometry Setup

During rendering, the solution draws the light cube first, then the textured ground plane, and finally the teapot. The geometry setup connects positions, normals, tangents, binormals, and texture coordinates to the matching shader attributes.
