Flat and Gouraud Shading

The flat and Gouraud variants both compute the Phong illumination model in the vertex shader. The difference is the interpolation qualifier: flat shading passes one color per primitive, while Gouraud shading interpolates the vertex colors across the triangle.

Phong Shading

For Phong shading, the vertex shader forwards view-space positions and normals. The fragment shader normalizes the interpolated normal and evaluates the ambient, diffuse, and specular terms per fragment, which produces smoother highlights.

Drawing the Plane, Teapot, and Light

Each frame updates the light animation, builds the view matrix, draws the white light cube, and then renders the plane and teapot with the selected shading program. The normal matrix is the transpose of the inverse model-view matrix, which keeps normals correct under transformations.
